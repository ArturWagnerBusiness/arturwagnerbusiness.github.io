(Return) My Games!

In between 2019 and 2020 I developed 2 small games which are playable in a browser. In 2024 I took my first ever Game Jam... and in 2025 my second...

What did the Stakeholder say again? [2025] (Jam Entry) (Play)
A game prototype developer for a Game Jam called Mini Jam 198: Speed. A 72 hour Jam was announced with a theme Speed. Once the limitation of "there is no end" was displayed, I got the idea for the game. This jam I tackled with an entirely different approach of first outlaying the entire gameplay in text, then scetch every screen and all gameplay elements. Surprisingly, although obvious at first glance, creating art, audio and code was a lot simpler then in my first jam or any project in that matter. I also got (most) features planned into the game. Hex MineSweeper [2024] (Source) (Play)
The main principle is the same as minesweeper. Numbers show the total of mines around the tile. The difference is that mines can have a value of more than 1. You can place numbered flags for assistance or destroy the flag with the mine underneath. The mines are shown at the top. Get them all to 0 and uncover all of the land. Aim for 0 hits if possible!

Wizy: The Time Wizard [2024] (Jam Entry) (Play)
A game prototype developed for a Game Jam called Mini Jame Gam #31. I decided to take part in this 59 hour Jam to see how well I could handle game development under strict time scales. Surprisingly I was able to finish the game a few hours before the deadline! Got in with no Godot knowledge but came out all and well.

Darkness within us [2020] (Source) (Play)
Explore the dark labyrinth, find keys and escape. Simple? Well, the issue is your light course is slowly fading... You need to recharge it... but to do so you need to go into the deep dark. Darkness in itself is not scary... but are you sure you are alone?
